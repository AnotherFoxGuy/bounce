79// A revolute joint constrains two bodies to share a point and an axis while
80// they are free to rotate about the point and the axis.
81// The relative rotation about the shared axis is the joint rotation.
82// You can limit the relative rotation with a lower and upper angle limit.
83// You can use a motor to drive the relative rotation about the shared axis.
